### Tips

#### Autocomplete

For Bash or Zsh, put something like this in your `$HOME/.bashrc` or `$HOME/.zshrc`:

```sh
# Pluralith
source <(pluralith completion zsh) # bash
```

```sh
source ~/.zshrc
```

## Common Spread Types

- Bitcoin Arbitrage: `BTCUSD-BTCEUR*EURUSD`
- Chart Inversions: `1/EURUSD`
- Currency Conversions: `BBY/EURUSD`
- Exchange Arbitrage: `BATS:FB-NASDAQ:FB`
- Instrument Comparisons: `AAPL/XAUUSD`
